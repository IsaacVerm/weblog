Research notes

Why a separate page for research notes? Why not just create another post? Sometimes I'm interested in exploring a topic in depth, but I'm not sure yet where things might lead. The topic might turn out to be a dead end or it might be very broad. That's why I need a place just to keep track of my research in these topics without having in mind a direct goal like I'd have for a post.

I've been keeping notes on topics I'm interested in for a while now, but kept these private. Problem doing this is they can't be shared that easily. By making them public, I can just share the link with anyone who might be interested.
